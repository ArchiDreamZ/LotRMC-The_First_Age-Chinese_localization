#title:翻腾之海

#types:hithlum,falas,doriath,nargothrond
#
（翻译累了！我要用机翻！）

这些荒芜之地的日子长了；
昔日的荣耀渐渐消退。
不久我将寻找银沙，
白色海鸥飞翔的避风港和水域。

我渴望微风轻拂，
展开帆以迎风。
不久，我将踏上起伏的大海，
抛开我青年时代的位置。

然后我将到达永生之地，
终于在闪亮的岸边下船了。
在提里奥站的青山上，
那里远离战争而令人愉快地休息。

Long grow the days on these forlorn lands;
Glory is fading from days of yore.
Soon I will seek for the silver sands,
Havens and waters where white gulls soar.

I long to feel the gentle breeze,
And unfurl sails to catch the wind.
Soon I will journey the rolling seas,
Leaving the place of my youth behind.

Then I will reach the Undying Lands,
At last alighting on shining shore.
To the green hills where Tirion stands,
There resting pleasantly far from war.
