#title:埃瑞西亚之树:瓦尔妲瑞安娜
#
#author:阿尔丹杜尔
#
#types:gondolin,nargothrond,hithlum,march_maedhros
#
#By Sir Lazuli (SirLazuli)
#
树干坚挺树枝长，绿叶欲滴嫩枝上。
花如九天亮银星，树似众神耀女王。
树上苍穹星闪烁，穹下巨树花琳琅。
芳香沁人绕树飞，飞入大地进心房。
不知何处花落下，密林深处隐人堂。
Stately limbs and slender twigs,
Leaves on graceful tender sprigs,
Flowers like a silver star,
Vardarianna of the Valar.
Stars in night sky glimmering,
Stars below them shimmering.
Pleasant fragrance wandring round,
Lingering over leafy ground,
Falling from the starry blooms,
In the forest’s vaulted rooms.
