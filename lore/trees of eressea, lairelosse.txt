#title:The Trees of Eressëa: Lairelossë
#
#author:Aldandur
#
#types:gondolin,nargothrond,hithlum,march_maedhros
#
#By Sir Lazuli (SirLazuli)
#
Silver leaf and branch below,
White like northern mountain snow.
On the Blessed Isle’s Elven-wood,
Shining Lairelossë stood.

All across the bright green lands,
Snowy trees in silver strands.
Beneath the boughs by warm air tossed,
Needles lie like fallen frost.
