#title:The Trees of Eressëa: Taniquelassë
#
#author:Aldandur

#types:gondolin,nargothrond,hithlum,march_maedhros

#By Sir Lazuli (SirLazuli)
#
Taniquelassë, towering tall tree,
Climbing the steep slopes, branching in splendour. 
Tower of mountains, among the loose scree,
Ever white soft leaves, silver trunks slender,
From Taniquetil, Valinor’s high peak,
Clothed in its white leaves through wintertime bleak.
